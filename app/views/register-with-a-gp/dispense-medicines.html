Dispensing medicines and appliances

Do you need your GP practice to dispense medicines and appliances?
What does this mean?

If you:

  • Live more than a mile from the nearest chemist
  • Would have serious difficulty in getting medicines and appliances from a chemist

Then you can ask for your GP practice to dispense to you instead. Note that not all practices are able to dispense.
