What’s a diabetes eye check?

A diabetes eye check looks for damage to your eyes which can lead to sight problems and possibly blindness:

  • you get drops in your eyes to enlarge your pupils (temporarily)
  • a photo will be taken of the back of your eye

Plan how you’ll get home after the check. Drops can make your eyes can be blurry and you shouldn’t drive for a couple of hours afterwards.
