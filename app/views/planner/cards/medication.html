Metformin helps keep your blood sugar levels under control.

More about Metformin

Metformin works by:

  • reducing the amount of glucose released into your blood
  • helping the cells remove glucose from the blood
  • reducing the amount of glucose you absorb from your food

Read more about Metformin at diabetes.org.uk

It can cause side effects. Speak to your GP if you have:

  • bloating
  • diarrhoea
  • feeling sick
