Prepare for your appointment:

  • wear your glasses or contact lenses
  • bring sunglasses (the drops can make your eyes sensitive to light)
  • plan how you’ll get home - your eyes can be blurry and you shouldn’t drive or travel on your own
What’s a diabetes eye check?

Diabetes can damage your blood vessels which can cause sight problems (diabetic retinopathy) and blindness. Eye checks can detect damage before you notice changes to your sight. Treating damaged blood vessels early can stop them affecting your sight.
