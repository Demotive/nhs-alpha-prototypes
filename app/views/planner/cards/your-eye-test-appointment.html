Why do I need an eye test?

Diabetes can damage your blood vessels which can cause sight problems (diabetic retinopathy) and blindness. Eye check can detect damage before you notice changes to your sight. Treating damaged blood vessels early can stop them affecting your sight.

Prepare for your appointment:

  • wear your glasses or contact lenses
  • bring sunglasses (the drops can make your eyes sensitive to light)
  • plan how you’ll get home - your eyes can be blurry and you shouldn’t drive or travel on your own
